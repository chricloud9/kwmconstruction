What is the Hero Program?

The Hero Program is a State of California approved program that allows Homeowners to make Energy Efficient improvements with NO money down. Upgrades are repaid through your tax bill with terms of 5 â€“ 25 years. The approved amount is based on the equity in your home with no credit requirements.

  • Transferability. Any balance may be able to transfer to new owner
